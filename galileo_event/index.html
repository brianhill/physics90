Galileo’s Lunar Observations

In The Starry Messenger, Galileo describes the Moon with the wonder of someone seeing its details for the first time in history. We will understand his observations and recreate his estimates of the Moon’s highest peaks. Participants should print out or have handy on a computer this PDF: Mountains on the Moon.
