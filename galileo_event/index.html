Galileo’s Lunar Observations

In The Starry Messenger, Galileo describes the Moon with the wonder of someone seeing its details for the first time in history. We will understand his observations and recreate his estimates of the Moon’s highest peaks. Participants should print out the worksheet posted here before the event.
