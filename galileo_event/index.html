Galileo’s Lunar Observations

In The Starry Messenger, Galileo describes the Moon with the wonder of someone seeing its details for the first time in history. We will understand his observations and recreate his estimates of the Moon’s highest peaks. Participants should print out or have handy on a computer this Worksheet: Galileo's Lunar Observations: Measuring the Mountains on the Moon.

For Seminar Informal Curriculum Credit turn your worksheet answers in to your Seminar professor.

Presentation: Galileo Measures the Mountains on the Moon, Profs. Brian Hill and Aaron Lee, Saint Mary's College of California, Seminar Informal Curriculum, April 30, 2020
