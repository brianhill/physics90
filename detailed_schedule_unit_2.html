Overall Unit 2 Outline (from Syllabus)

  • Unit 2: The Motions of the Earth, the Moon and the Planets. The scale of the Solar System. Kepler's Laws.
    • Note: A recurring theme in this unit…: In astronomy, objects are usually so big that you cannot get a tape measure very far around them. Or so far away that you have no hope of stretching a tape measure to them. How could you measure such circumferences or distances? Crafty use of trigonometry (staight lines, angles and triangles) is the recurring answer. In later units we will show astronomers' other crafty tricks.
